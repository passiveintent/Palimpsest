# Contributing

Palimpsest is currently source-available under the Business Source License 1.1
(`BUSL-1.1`).

Outside code contributions are not accepted unless they are covered by a signed
Contributor License Agreement (CLA) or another explicit written agreement with
the copyright holder. The agreement must grant the right to relicense,
commercially license, and otherwise distribute the contribution as part of
Palimpsest.

Issues, bug reports, design discussion, and documentation feedback are welcome.
Please do not submit pull requests containing code, tests, or other
copyrightable implementation material until a CLA process is available or you
have written permission from the copyright holder.

For contribution and commercial licensing questions, contact
purushottam@passiveintent.dev.
