## Honest accounting notes

- The baseline is per-instance remote-write (labels repeated every window, as RW 1.0 actually behaves), snappy-compressed with a real compressor; the zstd row is a generous baseline no stock Prometheus ships today.
- Palimpsest's ledger includes every escape hatch that fired: FALLBACK frames, dashcam snapshot blobs, dict deltas, golden re-announcements under churn.
- Dashcam snapshots here are per logical series (plsim's pipeline); the otel processor buffers per instance, so real snapshot bytes scale with flagged-series instance counts — see docs/ENVELOPE.md §6-8.
- Pod churn never touches the sketch (ADR-008): only the baseline pays label-set turnover. Logical churn is charged to Palimpsest via dict deltas and golden keyframes (ADR-017).
- Detection columns are measured against palimpsestd defaults; see docs/DEADZONE.md for why the leak is invisible and what it would take to see it.
