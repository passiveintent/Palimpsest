Generated by `plsim --deadzone` (seed 42). Full grid in `deadzone.csv`, heatmap in `deadzone.svg`; methodology and interpretation in `docs/DEADZONE.md`.

- sketch: m=2000, d=6, bits=8; 900 background series + 1 target
- storm: 25× rolling median over 30 windows; FALLBACK top-K 100
- recovery: FISTA iters=350 λ=0.05 power-iters=50 threshold=0.3; deviation events gated above solve residual 5

- **recovery floor**: smallest magnitude whose deviation event reliably emits (support hit, solve residual under the gate) at this and every larger tested magnitude.
- **detection floor**: same, but counting a FALLBACK heavy-hitter hit as detection when the storm breaker trips.
- **storm fires from**: smallest magnitude tripping the StormDetector in ≥50% of trials (— means never, i.e. the fallback breaker offers no help in this column).
- **undetected up to**: largest magnitude still missed by both paths in >50% of trials — the dead zone's measured top edge.
